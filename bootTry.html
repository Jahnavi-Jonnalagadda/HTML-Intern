What is Data Science?

"We have lots of data - now what?"

Data Science is the field of study that combines Domain Expertise, Programming Skills, and Knowledge of Mathematics and Statistics to extract meaningful insights out of data and solve analytically complex problems.



Unlike data in the traditional systems which was mostly structured, today most of the data is unstructured or semi-structured.
Businesses get flooded with data. Simple BI tools are not capable of processing huge volume and variety of data. The significance of Data Science is built on the fact that the existing kind of data seems not very useful.
And here is where Identifying and Refining data comes into picture.

How can we extract real value from data?

"Data Science is devoted to extracting clean information from raw data for the formulation of actionable insights."
